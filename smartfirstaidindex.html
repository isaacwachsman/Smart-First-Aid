Cut Instructions

Use pressure to apply a cloth or bandage on the wound for 20 to 30 minutes.
Clean the cut with cool, not cold, runninng water.
Use soap to clean around the wound.
If there are small objects in the wound, rinse them away with water or use tweezers to remove them.
